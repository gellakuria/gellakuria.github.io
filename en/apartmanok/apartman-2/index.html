Payments and refunds:

To secure your booking, you have to pay for the first part (usually 50%) within the first 5 days. You can transfer the money or use an OTP SZEP card. The remaining amount has to be paid on-site, we accept only cash and card. (on-site payment with SZEP card is not available at the moment, but we are working on it!).

We only accept HUF and EUR as currency.

 Not paying the first amount does not release you from the cancellation policy.

Cancellation:

The booking can be canceled for free 14 days before arrival. We will transfer the first part of the payment back.

If you cancel it between 14 and 3 days, 50% of your whole payment will be refunded. However, if you chose to pay the whole fee at once, we will keep 60% of your payment, these can be used within a year. This amount will be subtracted from the total amount of your next stay. If you cancel within 3 days, or you do not show up, we will charge the whole amount as a penalty. On-site reduction of nights, cancellation or not showing up at all will result in charging your total as a penalty.

Arrival and departure:

You can arrive between 3 PM and 6 PM, and you have to leave before 10 AM. If you wish to come earlier/ stay longer, let us know beforehand.
