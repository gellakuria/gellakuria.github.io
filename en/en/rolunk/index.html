About us

A wonderful place, full of love and lake Balaton. Have you ever seen or feel something like this? Now, you get a chance to live through it, experience it, taste it, grab it. Gella Kuria is located at the top of the "Old Gella", below Tótvázsony, just outside of Balatonszőlős, a perfect distance away from the sparkling life of Balatonfüred.

At the prettiest point of our Property, the gates of the Kali-basin always stay open. There is an amazing view of Tihany, the unique lake Balaton, that you are free to enjoy until the sun goes down, or as long as your eyes decide to give up. You can approach the Kuria from Tótvázsony, following an old asphalt road and a romantic path through the woods, which is marked by a green sign. At the end of the road, after a stone column, a car park will indicate that you have arrived. The accommodation which you can see today, with 6 rooms and 3 apartments was built in 1802, formed from a cellar-house, whose spectacular architecture you can enjoy to this day.

Our water comes from a 157 m deep well. If we enter through the front door, we end up at the reception desk. On the left side, you can find our common kitchen with a stove, oven, and a huge variety of basic kitchenware. You can also access the terrace and the common eating area. From the hall, you can access a common restroom. If you follow the corridor, you can find 2 separate entrances to double bedrooms. At the end of the corridor, you will see a bathroom with a shower and a bathtub. On the first floor, on the left side, we decided to set up a “family room”, a smaller double bedroom with separate entrances and a bathroom with a shower. On the right side, there are 2 separate double bedrooms, with a beautiful bathroom with a shower. We do have a mosquito net on all windows in the bedrooms. There is an option for our guests to grill or have a barbecue outside, with a roofed sitting area. We also have a wine cellar that can be visited during our organized winetasting tour. We do not use any chemicals, we want to protect nature, and we suggest you do too! Our accommodation is ideal if you wish to spend a few days in a calm environment, which is only noisy by nature. If you want to have a rest or feel nostalgic near the “untouched” lake Balaton, this place would be a great choice for you! If you want to travel with friends or family, we are waiting for you, have a great holiday here!
