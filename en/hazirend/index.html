House Rules

House Rules and IMPORTANT Facts:

In the area of the accomodation, smoking is strictly prohibited! Smoking is only allowed at the assigned places, outside of the area. Evidence of violation of our smoke free principle will result in a surcharge. Protect yout health and invironment: quit smoking! This accomodation is unfortunately unable to allow pets at this moment. We hope you find a solution for them and thus, neither You, or your small friend will be sad during these couple of days.

Check-in is possible between 3 PM and 6 PM. Checking out and leaving the accomodation should happen before 10 AM.

In the case of on-site reduction of nights,cancellation or not arriving at all, the total amount will be charged.

You can resort to the breakfast provided by us between 8.15 AM and 10. AM .

Quiet hours:
from 2 PM until 3 PM
from 10 PM to 8 AM

Please follow our rules and quiet hours, even in case of booking the whole accomodation. Pay attention to the calmness of the forest and the people surronding you! Please put the waste in the placed garbage cans. Pay attention to nature! Collect your waste selectively!

The drinking water supply of the EARTH is decreasing by leaps and bounds. Here in the mountains, the water has great value! Plese, do not waste it! Our water supply is provided by a 154m deep well. We do everything in our power to make sure that the water supply is persistent. Although it has not happened before, we may encounter some problems, considering the fact that we have our own water system. In such case, we ask you for your patience while we get rid of the problem. Please call us if the water keeps stalling and we will act rapidly in order to help you!

House rules for the Living room:

We kindly ask you to tidy up after yourself, make it comfortable for everyone! It is PROHIBITED to light a fire in the furnace without permission. Please avoid using the TV during quiet hours, or keep it at a low volume! If you are unable to set something up on the TV, ask for help! Please turn it off after use. Thank you.

House rules for the Kitchen:

You can use every item provided in the kitchen, but only for their proper use. Please wash them after use, and after drying or at least before leaving, put them back in their place. If you need help, we are at your service for a small amount of additional charge.

Please take care of the silverware, so the people coming after you can take good use of them as well.

Never leave the fireplace unattended. Turn off the gas-range and the oven, if you are not using them. They are FLAMMABLE! Thank you.

Please, do not leave anything that you brought here for cooking, or washing the dishes. Take them home.

If you cause serious damage in the kitchen or in the furniture, an additionnal charge must be paid.
